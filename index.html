About Me

I'm not your average developer. In fact, I'm not average at very many things. I make a habit of falling on one side of the spectrum or the other, be it my utter lack of fashion sense or my surprising aptitude for absorbing new concepts.

Even my background isn't average. Case in point: I used to be a mechanical engineer. At least, that was what I formally studied in college and - for two short years - practiced at Boeing Defense, where I worked on advanced military helicopters.

But then I quit that job and leveraged my engineering background to launch me into software development. Now, I'm a backend-leaning, full stack web developer who likes to dabble in new languages.

If you'd like to find out more about me, hover over the sidebar and check out my resume or recent projects, or just shoot me an email.
