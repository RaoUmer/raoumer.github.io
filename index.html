Research

My research focuses on developing next-generation foundation models for cancer diagnostics by integrating heterogeneous biomedical data and agentic AI systems. My goal is to advance translational healthcare by designing robust, scalable AI methods that improve diagnostic accuracy and support clinical decision-making. I have led and contributed to end-to-end AI projects, spanning model development, deployment, and interdisciplinary collaboration with clinical and research teams. I am particularly interested in building AI systems that bridge methodological innovation and real-world clinical impact, while fostering collaborative, open, and inclusive research environments.
