Research Interests

My research focuses on machine learning, i.e. in the development of algorithms capable of extracting hidden insight and knowledge from data. I'm particularly interested in machine learning and deep neural networks, mostly applied in the context of big data and to artificial intelligence problems such as information retrieval, natural language processing and computer vision.

More specifically, my research mainly addresses the following topics:

  • Tasks:

    • supervised, semi-supervised and unsupervised learning, structured output prediction, active learning, ranking;

  • Models:

    • SVMs, neural networks, deep learning, Markov random fields;

  • Applications:

    • document classification, information retrieval, object recognition;
