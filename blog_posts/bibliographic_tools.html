Bibliographic Tools

Find research papers and papers’ references:

  • SCI-HUB: mass & public access to research papers
  • Library Genesis: largest and longest running currently openly available collection
  • Google Scholar: freely accessible web search engine that indexes the full text or metadata of scholarly literature across an array of publishing formats and disciplines
  • Arxiv.org: Open access to 1,291,712 e-prints in Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Quantitative Biology, Quantitative Finance and Statistics
  • DOAJ: Directory of Open Access Journals
  • ISI web of knowledge: Ideal single research destination to explore the citation universe across subjects and around the world
  • CiteULike: citeulike is a free service for managing and discovering scholarly references
  • CiteSeerX alpha
  • Zotero: is a free, easy-to-use tool to help you collect, organize, cite, and share your research sources
  • Mendeley: Reference Management Software & Researcher Network
  • EndNote: is the industry standard software tool for publishing and managing bibliographies, citations and references
  • BibTeX: BibTeX is reference management software for formatting lists of references
  • CORE: Aggregating the world’s open access research papers
  • BASE: Academic search engine
  • Arxiv Sanity Preserver: Built in spare time by @karpathy to accelerate research.Serving last 33380 papers from cs.[CV|CL|LG|AI|NE]/stat.ML
  • BASE: Academic search engine
