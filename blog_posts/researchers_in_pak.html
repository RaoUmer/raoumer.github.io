Computer Scientist Researchers in Pakistan

Here is the curated list of currently working pioneer Computer Scientist reseachers in the field of Machine learning, Deep Learning and Data Science:

Dr. Fayyaz-ul-Amir Afsar Minhas


He is a senior scientist at the Department of Computer and Information Sciences (DCIS) at Pakistan Institute of Engineering and Applied Sciences (PIEAS), Pakistan. He is involved in active research and teaching in computer science.

He graduated with his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Colorado State University, Fort Collins, Colorado, USA under a Fulbright scholarship with Dr. Asa Ben-Hur. His primary area of research is machine learning in Bioinformatics.

He is currently looking for graduate (MS/MPhil and Ph.D.) students in computer science to work on problems in his newly established Bioinformatics Research Lab. If you are interested in research and development in these or related areas, please feel free to contact Dr. Fayyaz-ul-Amir Afsar Minhas.

For more information about Dr. Fayyaz-ul-Amir Afsar Minhas, please visit the following link: Dr. Fayyaz-ul-Amir Afsar Minhas

Dr. Asifullah Khan


He has more than 17 years of research experience and is working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at PIEAS. He is involved in active research and teaching in computer science.

He graduated with his Post-Doc in Computer Science from GIST, Gwangju, South Korea.His research interests include Deep Learning, image processing, and evolutionary algorithms.

For more information about Dr. Asifullah Khan, please visit the following link: Dr. Asifullah Khan

Dr. Muhammad Abid Mughal


He is a Principal scientist (Associate Professor) at the Department of Computer and Information Sciences (DCIS) at Pakistan Institute of Engineering and Applied Sciences (PIEAS), Pakistan. He is involved in active research and teaching in computer science.

He graduated with his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University P. R. China under a Higher Education Commission, Pakistan with Professor Wang DongSheng. His primary area of research is Data Science with focus on Big Data Analytics. Apart from this, his research interests also include: Big Data Systems and High Performance Computing.He is currently looking for graduate (MS/MPhil and Ph.D.) students in computer science to work on Data Science Analytics in diverse fields.

For more information about Dr. Muhammad Abid Mughal, please visit the following link: Dr. Muhammad Abid Mughal

Dr. Sibt ul Hussain


He is currently an Assistant Professor at National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES, FAST) Islamabad campus. His main research and teaching interests include deep learning, visual recognition (object detection, image classification, face recognition, etc.), machine learning and programming.

He was a postdoctoral researcher at University of Caen -- GREYC, working with Frederic Jurie in the area of visual recognition. Earlier, He spent five lovely years at Grenoble under the mentorship of Bill Triggs to complete his PhD thesis in Computer Vision (Visual Object Detection).

For more information about Dr. Sibt ul Hussain, please visit the following link: Dr. Sibt ul Hussain


Note: If you know any other researcher working in Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Data Science fields in Pakistan, Please, feel free to comment below with his/her own website link. I would like to add it in the above mentioned list.
