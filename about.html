About Me

I believe education is the only thing that can change this world. Knowledge is the most powerful asset that one can build through education. It builds up like compound interest. I am passionate about helping people through education. I care about unprivileged people, by sharing knowledge, and books. I always follow “Learn, Build, and Teach” strategy. I always try my best to learn something new, to build from it, and to teach it to others.

I'm a Lecturer in department of Computer Sciences and IT at The University of Lahore. Previously, I graduated in MS Computer Science in Computational Intelligence and Machine Learning from PIEAS . My under-graduate degree is in Computer System Engineering from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur , where I mainly worked on parallel and distributed computing and software engineering techniques.

Currently, I am mainly focus on Data Science and Applied Machine Learning problems. Learning from data in order to gain useful predictions and insights is a current major challenge for a Data Scientist.

Besides that, In spare time, I share my miscellaneous thoughts on my blog about Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science.

If you'd like to find out more about me, Please, have a look at my Statement of Purpose for more information.
