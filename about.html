About Me

Currently, I am a PhD student. Previously, I was a Lecturer in department of Computer Sciences and IT at The University of Lahore, Lahore, Pakistan. I completed my MS Computer Science degree in Computational Intelligence and Machine Learning from PIEAS , Islamabad, Pakistan. My under-graduate degree is in Computer System Engineering from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur , Bahawalpur, Pakistan. I am interested in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning, particularly in the low-level vision and image processing.

Besides that, I share my miscellaneous thoughts on my blog about Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science.
